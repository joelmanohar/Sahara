Life Insurance Claims After Death (India — IRDAI Guidelines):

OVERVIEW:
Life insurance is one of the most important financial benefits a family can claim after a death. IRDAI mandates that insurers settle non-early death claims within 30 days of receiving all documents. Early death claims (within 3 years of policy) may take up to 90 days for investigation.

TYPES OF DEATH CLAIMS:
1. Non-Early Claim: Death occurs after 3 years of policy commencement. Straightforward settlement.
2. Early Claim: Death occurs within 3 years. Insurer may investigate cause of death.
3. Accidental Death Claim: If policy has accidental death benefit rider, additional payout applies.

STEP 1: Locate the Policy Document
- Check physical files, email (search "policy", "premium", "LIC", "insurance"), bank statements for premium debits
- If policy document is lost: Contact insurer for duplicate. Online policies are accessible via insurer's portal.

STEP 2: Intimate the Insurance Company
- Call the insurer's claims helpdesk immediately
- Register an intimation online or at the nearest branch
- Note down the claim reference number

STEP 3: Collect Required Documents
FOR NOMINEE:
  - Claim Form (Form A / specific insurer form)
  - Death certificate (original or certified copy)
  - Original policy document (or indemnity bond if lost)
  - Nominee's identity proof (Aadhaar + PAN)
  - Nominee's photo
  - NEFT mandate form / cancelled cheque of nominee's bank account
  - Hospital records / treatment summary (if death due to illness)
  - FIR + Post-mortem report (if accidental/unnatural death)
  - Employer certificate (if group insurance)

IF NO NOMINEE:
  - Legal Heir Certificate or Succession Certificate
  - All documents listed above
  - Affidavit of all legal heirs with consent for one person to receive the claim

STEP 4: Submit Documents
- Submit at the nearest branch or through the insurer's online portal
- Get acknowledgment receipt with claim number and expected timeline

STEP 5: Claim Processing
- Non-early claims: 30 days from document submission
- Early claims: Up to 90 days (investigation may be conducted)
- If insurer rejects or delays: Written reason must be provided

STEP 6: Receive Payout
- Payment via NEFT to nominee's/legal heir's bank account
- Tax: Life insurance death claim payout is TAX-FREE under Section 10(10D) of Income Tax Act

HEALTH INSURANCE:
- If the deceased had a health insurance policy and incurred medical expenses before death:
  - File a health insurance claim for hospitalization costs
  - Submit: Hospital bills, discharge summary, death certificate, policy document
  - Timeline: 30 days as per IRDAI

ESCALATION:
- If claim is rejected: Request written reason. Appeal within 30 days.
- If delayed beyond timelines: Complain to IRDAI
  - IRDAI Grievance: igms.irda.gov.in
  - IRDAI Toll-free: 155255
  - Email: complaints@irdai.gov.in
- Insurance Ombudsman: For claims up to ₹30 lakh
  Link: https://www.cioins.co.in/

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Do NOT delay intimation. Inform the insurer within 24-48 hours if possible.
- Keep copies of all submitted documents.
- If multiple policies exist, file separate claims for each.
- Group insurance through employer: Contact HR department.
- Ensure NEFT details are correct to avoid payment delays.
