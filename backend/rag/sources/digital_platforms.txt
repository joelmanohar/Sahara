Digital Account Management After Death (India):

BANK ACCOUNTS (RBI Guidelines + Major Banks):
  Accounts ≤₹5L, no nominee: indemnity bond only (no succession cert required per RBI Master Circular)
  Docs: death cert ×3, claimant KYC, indemnity bond, passbook
  Processing: 7–15 working days
  Joint account survivor: death cert only
  Nominee: ID + death cert + claim form → 15 days
  Above ₹5L: Legal Heir Certificate or Succession Certificate
  Escalation: Banking Ombudsman if >30 days
  SBI: visit home branch, request "Claim Form for Deceased Account"
    Link: https://www.onlinesbi.sbi/
  HDFC Bank: call 1800-266-6161
    Link: https://www.hdfcbank.com/
  ICICI Bank: call 1800-200-3344
    Link: https://www.icicibank.com/

Life Insurance (IRDAI + LIC):
  Non-early claims: settle within 30 days (IRDAI mandate)
  Early claims (<3 yrs): max 90 days
  Docs: Form A (3783), death cert, original policy, claimant ID, NEFT mandate
  LIC: any branch, Download Form 3783
    Link: https://licindia.in/claims-services
  Contact: 022-68276827 | claimshelpdesk@licindia.in
  IRDAI Grievance: https://igms.irda.gov.in/
  Toll-free: 155255

EPF/PF (EPFO):
  Form 10D: monthly pension (widow/dependents)
  Form 20: lump-sum PF withdrawal
  Form 5IF: EDLI insurance claim (up to ₹7 lakh)
  Submit via employer HR or EPFO portal (if UAN active)
  Docs: death cert, UAN, nominee/heir details, bank proof
  Processing: 20 working days
  Portal: https://unifiedportal-mem.epfindia.gov.in/
  Helpline: 1800-118-005
  Grievance: https://epfigms.gov.in/

Demat/Stocks (SEBI Guidelines):
  With nomination: 7-day settlement
  Without: transmission form + death cert + succession cert + indemnity bond
  Below ₹5L market value: indemnity bond only
  Contact broker/DP directly for transmission form
  NSDL: https://www.nsdl.co.in/transmission.php
  CDSL: https://www.cdslindia.com/
  SEBI: https://www.sebi.gov.in/

Mutual Funds:
  With nominee: Submit death cert + nominee KYC + transmission form to AMC/registrar
  Without nominee: Legal heir certificate + consent letter from all heirs
  CAMS: https://www.camsonline.com/ (for most AMCs)
  KFintech: https://www.kfintech.com/ (for remaining AMCs)
  Process: 7-15 working days
  Important: Units are transmitted, not redeemed. Tax applies only on future redemption.

PPF (Public Provident Fund):
  Nominee receives balance + interest up to date of death
  If no nominee: Legal heir with succession certificate
  Submit at the Post Office or bank where PPF is held
  Docs: death cert, nominee ID, PPF passbook, application form
  Process: 15-30 working days
  Link: https://www.indiapost.gov.in/

NPS (National Pension System):
  Pre-retirement death: Entire corpus to nominee as lump sum
  Post-retirement death: Depends on annuity type chosen
  Submit claim through POP (Point of Presence)
  Link: https://www.npscra.nsdl.co.in/

UPI Wallets:
  No formal nomination system in India for wallets
  Contact support with death cert + claimant ID
  PhonePe: support.phonepe.com → deceased account report
    Link: https://support.phonepe.com/
  Google Pay: Google account support
    Link: https://support.google.com/googlepay/
  Paytm: care@paytm.com subject "Deceased Account – [Phone]"
    Link: https://paytm.com/care/
  Important: Transfer any wallet balance BEFORE deactivating mobile SIM

Email Accounts:
  Gmail: Inactive Account Manager or deceased user request
    Link: https://support.google.com/accounts/troubleshooter/6357590
  No account access given; only closure/data download possible
  Google Takeout (for data before closure): https://takeout.google.com/
  Outlook/Microsoft: deceased user request form
    Link: https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/concern/deceased
  Yahoo: Send death cert + proof to Yahoo support
  Processing: 2-4 weeks

Social Media:
  Facebook: memorialization or removal request
    Memorialization: https://www.facebook.com/help/1506822589577997
    Removal: https://www.facebook.com/help/228813257197480
  Instagram: removal only (no memorialization for Instagram)
    Link: https://help.instagram.com/264154560391256
  Twitter/X: deactivation request
    Link: https://help.twitter.com/forms/privacy
  LinkedIn: memorialization request
    Link: https://www.linkedin.com/help/linkedin/answer/a1342062
  WhatsApp: unlink SIM; auto-deletes after 120 days of inactivity
  YouTube: Covered under Google Account closure

Subscriptions and Recurring Payments:
  List from bank statements and UPI AutoPay
  Contact each service's support
  Most will cancel + provide pro-rata refund with death cert
  Cancel UPI AutoPay via bank's UPI app
  Common services to check:
    - Netflix, Amazon Prime, Disney+, Spotify
    - Newspapers/magazines subscriptions
    - Cloud storage (Google One, iCloud, OneDrive)
    - Domain names, web hosting
    - Gym memberships
    - OTT platforms

Vehicle Registration (RTO):
  Submit Form 31 to RTO for transfer
  Docs: death cert, legal heir cert, RC book, insurance, PUC
  If vehicle loan exists: NOC from bank after loan settlement
  Link: https://parivahan.gov.in/
  Process: 15-30 working days
